Brittany Steenbergen was born in Davis, CA and lived there her whole life until coming to Concordia University Irvine to pursue a degree in computer science. During her years at Davis Senior High School, she took three years of AP Studio Art where she took an interest in acrylic painting. Over the three years her portfolio grew immensly and now she has a wide variety of landscape, abstract, and surrealist paintings.
